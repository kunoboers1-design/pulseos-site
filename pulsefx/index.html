About

Important details.

Description

PulseFX is built for traders who want to calculate position size quickly and keep their accounts organized.

With a fast lot size calculator, account tracking, alerts, notes, widgets, and simple reports, the app helps reduce friction in the trading workflow.

Whether you manage funded accounts or your own capital, you can track balances, review progress, and keep trading context in one place.
