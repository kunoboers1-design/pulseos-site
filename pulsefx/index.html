About

Important details.

Description

PulseFX is built for traders who want to calculate position sizes quickly and keep their accounts organized.

With a simple but powerful lot size calculator, account tracking, widgets, and structured summaries, the app saves time and keeps trading organized.

Whether you manage phase accounts, funded accounts, or your own capital, you can monitor balances, set goals, and connect trades to notes and photos.
