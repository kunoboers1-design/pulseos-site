Overview

App Name PulseFX - Lot Size Calculator
Target Audience Traders
Short Description A mobile app designed to simplify lot size management while providing notifications and clear trading statistics.

Description

PulseFX is built for traders who want to calculate position sizes quickly and keep their accounts organized.

With a simple yet powerful lot size calculator, account tracking, and widgets for instant insights, the app saves time and keeps your trading structured.

Whether you’re managing phase accounts, funded accounts, or your own capital, you can monitor balances, set goals, and link trades to notes and photos. Automatic daily and weekly summaries help you stay clear on your progress.

Key Features

Lot Size Calculator

  • Instantly calculate the correct lot size based on your chosen risk
  • Compact mode for quick calculations
  • Open MetaTrader within the app

Account Management

  • Add multiple accounts, track balances, mark favorites, and set optional goals
  • Free up to 5 accounts; more than 5 requires Premium
  • View equity curve (Premium)

Trade Notes

  • Link trades to notes, screenshots, or photos for a structured journal

Mini Statistics Dashboard

  • Win rate, best and worst trades, average lot size, P/L since reset, most used pairs, and more

Daily & Weekly Reports

  • Automatic notifications with your risk/reward overview
  • Daily reports are free; weekly reports are available with Premium

Widgets

  • Last Lot Size
  • Account Overview (Premium)
  • Upcoming Alerts (Premium)

How the App Works

1

Download & Setup

Available in the App Store.

2

Add Account

  • Select account type: funded, own capital, or phase
  • Enter your starting balance
3

Risk Calculator

  • Select trading pair, entry, and stop loss
  • The app instantly calculates your lot size and risk
  • Open your trade in MetaTrader with one tap
  • After closing your trade, return to the app and enter your P/L
  • Add your notes about the trade
  • Repeat with your updated balance
4

Statistics

  • View your equity curve
  • Track daily and weekly results
  • Access a mini statistics dashboard
5

Notifications

  • Receive a daily notification at 20:00 with your R/R update
  • Customize reminders for trading sessions, goals, or news events
  • Get notified when you reach the goal set for your account
