About

Important details.

Description

Pulse Habits is designed for people who want to become more disciplined without turning habit tracking into noise.

It focuses on identity, momentum, and repeatability — helping habits feel more meaningful and more sustainable over time.

Privacy

Pulse Habits is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal information.

Your habits, streaks, and progress data stay on your device. We do not use ad networks or external tracking tools.
