Story

How it started.

2022

Trading got me hooked.

In early 2022 I got into trading and became completely obsessed. Studying charts, strategies, risk management — I was all in. But after a while I realized I couldn't put all my eggs in one basket. I needed more.

2023

Notion, templates, and building things.

I discovered Notion and instantly loved it. I started building templates and systems — tried selling them online, but that never really took off. Mostly gave them away to friends. Still, something clicked: I genuinely liked creating tools that help people.

2024

First app. First spark.

App development always interested me, but traditional programming felt too slow and frustrating. Then I realized — I was already using AI tools every day. Why not combine that with building apps? So I did. I shipped my first app: FX Lotsize Notifier. And I was hooked immediately.

Now

PulseOS.

More ideas, more apps. What started as a single tool for traders became a full system. Since early 2026 I'm also back in school studying Data Analysis — because I want to keep growing in every direction. PulseOS is the project I'm building alongside all of that.

Drive
"What drives me is taking an idea that lives in your head and turning it into something real — something other people can actually use, and enjoy."

That feeling never gets old. Every app in PulseOS started as a thought. Building it out, seeing it work, and knowing someone benefits from it — that's exactly why I keep going.
