Support

Need help with a PulseOS app?

Reach out for support, bug reports, feedback, or suggestions.

Email

For support, technical questions, or general inquiries, contact:

support@pulseos.eu

We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.

What to include

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

  • The app name (for example: PulseFX)
  • Your device model (iPhone, iPad, or Mac)
  • Your iOS or macOS version
  • A short description of the issue or suggestion

Feedback

Feedback, ideas, and feature suggestions are always welcome. PulseOS apps are actively developed, and user input helps improve performance, reliability, and usability.

Apps

Support may relate to any PulseOS application, including:

  • PulseFX
  • Pulse Habits
  • Pulse Recipes
  • Future PulseOS apps
