Privacy Policy.

PulseOS apps are designed to keep data usage minimal, clear, and local to your device.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

PulseOS respects your privacy. Our apps are built to provide useful functionality without unnecessary data collection, intrusive tracking, or advertising profiles.

What we collect

PulseOS apps are designed to work primarily on your device. Information you enter — such as settings, account values, notes, or preferences — stays local unless clearly stated otherwise.

We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information through our apps.

How data is used

App data is used only to provide the features of the app and support the experience you expect from the product.

We do not use your data to build marketing profiles or serve targeted advertising.

Analytics and tracking

PulseOS keeps analytics and tracking to a minimum. We do not use intrusive ad networks or external tracking tools to monitor your personal behavior across apps or websites.

Third parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

If a PulseOS app uses third-party platform services such as Apple systems or app distribution tools, those services are governed by the privacy terms of the relevant platform provider.

Data security

We aim to keep app experiences simple and privacy-conscious. However, no device, software environment, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Contact

Privacy-related questions? Reach out at support@pulseos.eu

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated over time as PulseOS grows. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
