Privacy

Your data stays simple.

PulseOS apps are designed to keep data usage minimal, clear, and as local to your device as possible.

Overview

PulseOS respects your privacy. Our apps are built to provide useful functionality without unnecessary data collection, intrusive tracking, or advertising profiles.

What we collect

In general, PulseOS apps are designed to work primarily on your device. Information you enter into the app, such as settings, account values, notes, or app preferences, is intended to remain local unless clearly stated otherwise.

We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information through our apps.

How data is used

App data is used only to provide the features of the app, improve usability, and support the experience you expect from the product.

We do not use your data to build marketing profiles or serve targeted advertising.

Analytics and tracking

PulseOS aims to keep analytics and tracking to a minimum. We do not use intrusive ad networks or external tracking tools to monitor your personal behavior across apps or websites.

Third parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

If a PulseOS app uses third-party platform services such as Apple systems or app distribution tools, those services are governed by the privacy terms of the relevant platform provider.

App-specific privacy

Some PulseOS apps may include more specific privacy details depending on their features. Where applicable, the app-specific privacy information or in-app policy takes precedence.

Data security

We aim to keep app experiences simple and privacy-conscious. However, no device, software environment, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Contact

Changes

This privacy information may be updated over time as PulseOS grows and changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
