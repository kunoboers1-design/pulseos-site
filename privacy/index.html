Privacy

Your data stays simple.

PulseOS apps are designed to keep things as local, private, and minimal as possible.

Introduction

At PulseOS, we respect your privacy. Our apps are designed to keep things simple and avoid unnecessary data collection wherever possible.

What we collect

In general, PulseOS apps are built to work primarily on your device. Data you enter into the apps is intended to remain local unless explicitly stated otherwise.

We do not aim to collect unnecessary personal information, use intrusive tracking, or build advertising profiles.

How data is used

Any data used by a PulseOS app is used to provide the app’s features, improve usability, and support the experience you expect from the product.

Third parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

App-specific privacy

Some apps may have their own more detailed privacy information. When that applies, the app-specific privacy page or in-app policy takes precedence.

Changes

This privacy information may be updated over time as PulseOS grows. Changes will be reflected on this page.
