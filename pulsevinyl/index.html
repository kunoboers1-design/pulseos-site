Features

What PulseVinyl gives you.

Vinyl Availability Scan

Scan your entire Apple Music library and instantly see which albums are available on vinyl.

  • Full library scan
  • Powered by Discogs database
  • Availability badge per album

Owned & Wanted

Track your vinyl collection. Mark albums as owned or add them to your wanted list and see everything at a glance.

  • Separate Owned and Wanted tabs
  • Collection overview at a glance
  • Up to 10 of each for free

Barcode Scanner

Scan the barcode on a physical vinyl record to look it up instantly on Discogs and add it to your collection.

  • Physical record lookup
  • Add to Owned or Wanted
  • 5 free scans per month

Collection Value

Track the estimated market value of your vinyl collection based on Discogs pricing data.

  • Estimated total value
  • Per-record pricing
  • Powered by Discogs marketplace

Release Radar

Discover upcoming vinyl releases and pre-orders from artists already in your Apple Music library.

  • Personalized to your library
  • Pre-order alerts
  • Never miss a release

Export & CSV

Export your full collection as a CSV file to back it up, share it, or use it in any spreadsheet app.

  • Full collection export
  • Compatible with any spreadsheet app
  • Available with Pro

How it works

Simple from start to collection.

1

Connect Apple Music

Grant read access to your Apple Music library. No login needed, no data leaves your device.

2

Scan for vinyl

PulseVinyl checks each album in your library against the Discogs database to find vinyl releases.

3

Build your collection

Mark albums as owned, add them to your wanted list, and track your collection over time.

Powered by Discogs

The world's largest vinyl database.

Discogs API

Connected to 8 million+ releases

PulseVinyl uses the Discogs API to check vinyl availability, look up barcodes, and estimate collection value. You can connect your own Discogs account to enable additional features and sync your collection data.
