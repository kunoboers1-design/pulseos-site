Features

What you can do with PulseLifts.

Log workouts fast

Build workouts from your exercise library and log every set, rep, and weight as you go. No clutter, no distractions.

  • Custom workout builder
  • Quick set and rep logging
  • Rest timer between sets

Track personal records

Every time you hit a new personal best, PulseLifts tracks it automatically. See your all-time records per exercise at a glance.

  • Auto-detected PRs per exercise
  • 1RM estimation
  • PR history over time

Exercise library

Browse exercises by muscle group, equipment, or category. Add custom exercises for anything not in the default library.

  • Filter by muscle group
  • Filter by equipment
  • Add custom exercises

Measure progress over time

See how your strength, volume, and frequency develop week by week. Progress that stays visible keeps you motivated.

  • Volume and load tracking
  • Workout frequency overview
  • Full session history

About

Description

PulseLifts is the gym tracking app in the PulseOS ecosystem. Log workouts, track exercises, measure personal records, and see your progress build over time.

Built for people who train with intent and want a clean, fast app that stays out of the way.

Privacy

PulseLifts is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal information.

Your workouts, records, and progress data stay on your device. We do not use ad networks or external tracking tools.
