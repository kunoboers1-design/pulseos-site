Features

What you can do with Pulse Recipes.

Daily inspiration

The home screen shows recipes based on the time of day — breakfast ideas in the morning, dinner inspiration in the evening. Always relevant, zero effort.

  • Time-based recipe suggestions
  • Breakfast, lunch, dinner and snack rails
  • Quick shortcuts to browse or shop

Import from any recipe site

Paste a URL and save the full recipe with ingredients, steps, photo, and macros. Works best with English and Dutch recipe pages that include structured data.

  • URL import with auto-detection
  • Review and edit before saving
  • Saves ingredients, steps, and photo

Track macros, rate, and repeat

See kcal, protein, carbs, and fat per recipe — scalable per serving. Rate meals on taste and overall quality, mark whether you'd eat it again, and log when you last cooked it.

  • Kcal, protein, carbs, fat — per serving
  • Taste and overall star ratings
  • "Would eat again" toggle + last eaten tracking

Shopping lists from your recipes

Pick a recipe, get a complete ingredient list ready to check off in the store. Items are grouped and amounts scale with your chosen servings.

  • Auto-generated from recipe ingredients
  • Amounts scale with servings
  • Check off items as you shop

Free to start, premium for more

Start for free with up to 20 recipes. Upgrade once for unlimited recipes and iCloud sync across all your devices. No subscription, no recurring costs — just a one-time purchase.

  • 20 recipes free, no time limit
  • One-time purchase for unlimited recipes
  • iCloud sync included with premium

About

Important details.

Description

Pulse Recipes helps you save, organize, and cook your favorite recipes. Import from any website, track macros, rate meals, and generate shopping lists — all in one app.

Free to use with up to 20 recipes. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited recipes and iCloud sync across devices. One-time purchase, no subscription.

Privacy

Pulse Recipes is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal information. Premium users can enable iCloud sync to keep recipes available across devices.

Your recipes, ingredients, and meal data stay on your device. We do not use ad networks or external tracking tools.
