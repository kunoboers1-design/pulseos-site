Features

What you can do with PulseReflect.

Daily reflection prompts

Start each day with a focused prompt designed to help you slow down, think clearly, and gain perspective on what matters.

  • New prompt every day
  • Morning and evening modes
  • Skip or save for later

Free journal writing

Write freely without prompts when you need to process something. Clean, distraction-free editor designed for honest writing.

  • Unlimited entries
  • Distraction-free writing mode
  • Search across all entries

Mood tracking

Log how you feel each day with a simple mood check-in. See patterns in your mindset over days, weeks, and months.

  • Daily mood check-in
  • Mood trends over time
  • Connect mood to entries

Weekly review

Look back at your week's entries and mood patterns. Understand what worked, what didn't, and what to focus on next.

  • Weekly summary view
  • Streak and consistency tracking
  • Monthly look-back

About

Description

PulseReflect is the journaling and self-reflection app in the PulseOS ecosystem. Daily prompts, free writing, mood tracking, and weekly reviews — all in one clean app.

Built for people who want to think more clearly, process their days honestly, and grow through self-awareness over time.

Privacy

PulseReflect is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal information.

Your journal entries, mood logs, and reflection data stay on your device. We do not use ad networks or external tracking tools.
